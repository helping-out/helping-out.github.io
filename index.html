DRIVES CONDUCTED

During this time of distress, we all must extend our support those who are not so privileged. A majority of people living in slums are daily wage labourers who survive on meagre earnings. With the country under lockdown, their earning has come to a stop and has left them to starve. Even if they are protected from coronavirus, who will save them from starvation?

It is now in our hands to do whatever we can to help those around us. As we step out of the gate of our complex, there are slums on both the sides, towards science city and mukundapur. We plan to collect funds, buy dry foods (dals, rice etc.) and distribute these among them.

HELPING OUT

HOW CAN I HELP?

DRIVES CONDUCTED

At this time of distress, we urbanites must come together and extend our support those who are not so privileged. The whole country is on lockdown, a majority of peopple living in slums are daily wage labourers who survive on their daily earnings. With the country on lockdown, their earning has come to a stop and has left them to starve. Even if they are protected from coronavirus, who will save them from starvation?

It is now in our hands to come together as humans and do whatever we can to help those around us. As we step out of the gate of our complex, there are slums on both the sides, towards science city and mukundapur. We plan to collect funds, buy dry foods (dals, rice etc.) and distribute these among them.
